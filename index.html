Safety, Security & Comfort Guaranteed

Haven Structures utilizes cutting edge design technology to design and develop insulated, modular, and blast-resistant fabric buildings that can be customized for your particular need and application. Inline with API RP 756 recommendations.

Use Cases

Mobile Offices

Workers losing time and productivity on-site because of long walks back to the site office? Our tents can be picked up by a crane and moved around the site - and are insulated.

Hot Works

Our Blast-Proof Tents fit comfortably over pressurized Hot Works Tents, and can contain larger hot-works areas, closer to the work center.

Break Tent

Our blast-resistant tents meet API RP 756 - which means they can be located in Zones 1 & 2 - increasing worker safety and productivity.

Warehousing

We can design warehousing for your customized specification. Just contact us to let us know what you need.
